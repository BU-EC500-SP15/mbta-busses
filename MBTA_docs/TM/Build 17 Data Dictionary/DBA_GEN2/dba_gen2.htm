Technical Reference : Database Administration : TM 02

 

This documentation contains the accepted requirements and procedures for proper administration of the SQL database utilized by the TransitMaster system. While other configurations and procedures may be adopted by individual properties, they may not be acceptable to the TransitMaster system. TransitMaster makes no guarantees on procedures developed by individual properties. TransitMaster reserves the right to amend this requirement and procedure list as new data becomes available. Also note that although TransitMaster makes every attempt to ensure the recovery of the system, it cannot anticipate nor be responsible for hardware malfunction which may occur during the backup process. It is therefore recommended that all redundancy items factored into the backup process be followed to aid in curbing the effect of such malfunctions.

These guidelines assume that 30 days of message detail and 12 months of summary data will be retained on-line. Individual questions or concerns will be addressed on a case by case basis. TransitMaster personnel will complete the initial implementation of the procedures detailed herein. Training will be provided on site so that the local property system administrator can support the backup tasks.
